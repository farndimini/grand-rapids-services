24/7 Emergency Home Services in Grand Rapids

Emergencies don't follow a 9-to-5 schedule, and neither do we. Grand Rapids Home Services provides round-the-clock emergency service for urgent home repair needs across West Michigan.

Our Emergency Services

  • Emergency Plumbing — Burst pipes, sewer backups, water heater failure, gas leaks
  • Emergency Electrical — Power outages, sparking outlets, breaker panel issues, exposed wiring
  • Emergency HVAC — No heat in winter, no AC in summer, refrigerant leaks, thermostat failures
  • Water Damage — Flooded basements, storm damage, water extraction, drying services
  • Emergency Roofing — Storm damage, leaks, fallen tree damage, temporary tarping
  • Garage Door Emergencies — Broken springs, stuck doors, opener failures

When to Call for Emergency Service

If you're experiencing any of these situations, call (616) 555-0147 immediately:

  • Water leaking or flooding that can cause structural damage
  • Complete loss of heating in winter or cooling in summer
  • Electrical sparking, smoking outlets, or partial power loss
  • Sewage backup or gas odor
  • Garage door that won't open or close (safety risk)
  • Storm damage with exposed interior or active leaking
