Our Warranties & Guarantees

We stand behind every job we do. Grand Rapids Home Services offers comprehensive warranties and guarantees on all workmanship and materials.

Satisfaction Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with our work, we'll make it right. No questions asked. Our satisfaction guarantee covers every service we provide — from a simple faucet repair to a complete kitchen remodel.

Workmanship Warranty

  • Repairs: 1-year warranty on all labor
  • Installations: 2-year warranty on workmanship
  • Major Remodels: 3-year warranty on all construction work
  • Emergency Services: 90-day warranty on emergency repairs

Manufacturer Warranties

All parts and materials we install carry the full manufacturer's warranty. We only use quality materials from trusted brands, and we'll help you navigate any manufacturer warranty claims if issues arise.

What's Covered

  • Workmanship defects — If something fails due to improper installation, we fix it free
  • Material defects — Manufacturer defects in parts and materials are covered by their warranty
  • Follow-up adjustments — Free adjustments within 30 days of service

How to Make a Warranty Claim

If you experience an issue with work we've performed, simply call (616) 555-0147. We'll schedule a service call to diagnose and resolve the issue at no charge if it's covered under warranty.
