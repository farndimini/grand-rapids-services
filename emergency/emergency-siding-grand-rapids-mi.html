Emergency Siding Checklist

If you're facing an emergency, follow these steps while waiting for our technician to arrive.

  • Turn off the system or water supply if safe to do so
  • Check your breaker panel or shut-off valve
  • Take photos of visible damage for insurance
  • Gather model/serial numbers from equipment
  • Clear access to the work area
  • Have your ID and proof of ownership ready
  • Note any unusual sounds, smells, or error codes
  • Keep pets secured during service visit
