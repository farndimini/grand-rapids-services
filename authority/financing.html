Financing Options for Your Home Service Projects

We believe that quality home services should be accessible to every Grand Rapids homeowner. That's why we offer flexible financing options to help you manage the cost of your project.

Available Financing Options

  • No Money Down — Start your project with $0 down payment
  • Low Monthly Payments — Spread the cost over affordable monthly payments
  • Fixed Interest Rates — Lock in a fixed rate with predictable monthly payments
  • Flexible Terms — Choose 6, 12, 24, or 36-month payment plans
  • No Prepayment Penalty — Pay off your balance early with no extra fees

How It Works

  1. Call (616) 555-0147 for a free estimate on your project
  2. Ask about financing options during your consultation
  3. Complete a quick application (takes about 5 minutes)
  4. Get approved and schedule your service
