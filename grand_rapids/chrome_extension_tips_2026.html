Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best Chrome extension for productivity?
Todoist is a popular choice for task management and productivity.
Are Chrome extensions safe to install?
Most Chrome extensions are safe to install, but it's essential to read reviews and check the developer's reputation before installing.
Do Chrome extensions slow down your browser?
Some Chrome extensions may slow down your browser, but most extensions are designed to be lightweight and efficient.
What is Manifest V3 and how does it affect extensions?
Manifest V3 is a new version of the Chrome extension manifest file that improves security and performance. It may require developers to update their extensions.
How many Chrome extensions should I have installed?
It's recommended to have only the extensions you need, as too many extensions can slow down your browser.

Hidden Downsides of Chrome Extensions

Some Chrome extensions may have hidden costs, such as:
  • Setup fees: Some extensions may require a one-time payment or subscription.
  • Mandatory upgrades: Some extensions may require you to upgrade to a newer version, which may have additional costs.
  • Usage-based pricing: Some extensions may charge based on usage, which can add up over time.
It's essential to read the terms and conditions before installing an extension.
