<h2 id="ublock-origin">uBlock Origin</h2>
**Rating: 9.5/10**
The pricing for uBlock Origin is $0.00, as it is a free and open-source extension. 
What it does well:
- Blocks ads and trackers with high efficiency
- Customizable filter lists for advanced users
- Low system resource usage
Where it falls short:
- Can be overwhelming for novice users due to its complex settings
- Some websites may not function properly due to over-blocking
- Limited support for non-English languages
Who it is best for: Advanced users looking for a robust ad blocker.

**Rating: 7.5/10**

What it does well:
- Automatically applies coupon codes at checkout
- Provides price history and price alerts
- Supports a wide range of online stores
Where it falls short:
- May not work on all websites or with all coupon codes
- Can be slow to update price information
- Limited customer support
Who it is best for: Online shoppers looking for a convenient way to save money.
