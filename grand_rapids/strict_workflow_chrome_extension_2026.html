Strict Workflow

Rating: 8.5/10

Pricing: The Strict Workflow extension is free to download and use, with no premium tiers or subscriptions. However, the developer accepts donations to support the extension's development and maintenance.

What it does well:

  • Blocks distracting websites and applications
  • Tracks time spent on tasks and projects
  • Allows for customization of the blocklist and whitelist

Where it falls short:

  • Lack of a mobile app
  • Limited reporting features
  • No integration with other productivity tools

Who it is best for: Strict Workflow is ideal for individuals who need help staying focused and avoiding distractions while working on their computers. It is particularly useful for students, freelancers, and remote workers who require a simple yet effective tool to boost their productivity.

Freedom

Rating: 9.0/10

Pricing: Freedom offers a free trial, after which it costs $6.99/month or $29.99/year.

What it does well:

  • Blocks distracting websites and applications across all devices
  • Tracks time spent on tasks and projects
  • Offers advanced reporting features

Where it falls short:

  • More expensive than Strict Workflow
  • Can be complex to set up and use
  • No customization options for the blocklist and whitelist

Who it is best for: Freedom is ideal for individuals who require a more advanced and comprehensive tool to boost their productivity. It is particularly useful for professionals and teams who need to track their time and stay focused across multiple devices.
