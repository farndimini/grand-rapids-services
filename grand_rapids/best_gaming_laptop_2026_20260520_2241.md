<h2 id="asus-rog-zephyrus-g15">ASUS ROG Zephyrus G15</h2>
<p><b>Rating: 9.2/10</b></p>
<p>Pricing: The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G15 starts at $1,499 for the base model with the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 GPU, 16 GB of RAM, and a 512 GB SSD. The top-of-the-line model with the RTX 4080 GPU, 32 GB of RAM, and a 1 TB SSD costs $2,499.</p>
<p>The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G15 excels in several areas, including its exceptional display quality, with a 15.6-inch 4K IPS panel featuring a 120Hz refresh rate and a 3ms response time. Furthermore, the laptop's AI-powered cooling system adjusts fan speeds and temperatures based on the current workload, ensuring optimal performance and minimal noise.</p>
<p>However, the Zephyrus G15 falls short in a few areas. Its battery life is limited, with an average of 4-5 hours of gameplay on a single charge. Additionally, the laptop's RAM is not easily upgradable, which may be a concern for users who want to future-proof their device.</p>
<p>The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G15 is best for gamers who prioritize high-performance graphics and a premium display. However, users who value long battery life or easy upgradability may want to consider alternative options.</p>
